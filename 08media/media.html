Event Types

Event Type Description Duration of Event
Dinner Party A sit-down meal with a small group of guests, focused on good food, relaxed conversation, and a comfortable table setup. Works well for celebrating special occasions or simply hosting friends. 2-3 hours
Girls/Boys Night A casual hangout with close friends, often centered around snacks, music, movies, crafts and activities or drinks. It's all about unwinding, bonding, and enjoying a low-pressure evening with friends. 3+ hours
Game Night A fun, interactive event built around board games, card games, group activities, or video games. Ideal for lively energy, snacks, and flexible seating so guests can gather around and have a fun time. 2-3 hours
Birthday Party A celebration centered on a guest of honor, typically including food, a cake, decorations, and activities that bring friends and family together. Can be formal or relaxed depending on the theme. 3+ hours
Kids Birthday Party A playful, high-energy celebration with age-appropriate games, crafts, and snacks. Planning usually focuses on safety, supervision, and creating a fun environment that keeps children engaged. 2-3 hours
Themed Party A gathering built around a specific idea, aesthetic, or concept — such as a decade ('80s), color palette, holiday, or movie. Decorations, food, and activities all reflect the chosen theme to create a memorable experience and gather friends and family. 2-4 hours
